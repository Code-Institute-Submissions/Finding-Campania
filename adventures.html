Valle delle Ferriere

Description

A beautiful hike with a combination of strenuous climbs and flat trails. Over the course of your trail, you will come across forest, cliffs, old houses and waterfalls. Waterfalls are perfect place to have that picnic lunch you are carrying. Although Valle delleFerriere and Valle deiMulini are in the same valley, the hike trails are completely different and is a must do while at Amalfi!
